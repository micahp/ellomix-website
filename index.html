ellmix

əˈlōmiks n.

ellomix is the ultimate music social media app.

The Features

Timeline

Users will have the ability to share music content for friends to view, like, repost, comment, and more. Easy way to stay updated with the hottest music your friends, DJ’s, and favorite artists are listening to.

Integration

Users will have the ability to sync other music app platforms. This will enable users to have once place save all of their music history and will be used to recommend new music.

Messaging

Users will have the ability to message privately to a single user or group chat. User can share music and write messages in this function.

Playlists

Users will be able to create playlists in addition to just single songs with friends.
